ABOUT

Welcome to our website. Brookes & Sons window cleaners are a specialist commercial window cleaning company based in Manchester and Greater Manchester. Our reach and wash system enables us to tackle absolutely any requirement on a contract or one off basis. Our team of Manchester based window cleaners are available to give you a free no obligation quotation no matter how big or small your requirements may be.

Window cleaning has changed over the years with the introduction of reach and wash systems. Our window cleaners in Manchester are specialists in reach and wash window cleaning systems. Reach and wash all but eliminates the need for ladders and dramatically reduces health and safety risks. When working on highly populated sites, this system has proven itself thousands of times over. As well as commercial window cleaning in Manchester we can also clean guttering, signs, cladding and we also complete a great detail of internal window cleaning.
